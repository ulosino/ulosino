[Solus](https://getsol.us) is a Linux distribution built from the ground up. It aims to be a complete package, targeting the general computing markets. Solus ships it's flagship GNOME 2 style desktop called Budgie by default, and even though it works with many GNOME libraries, the team at Solus have gone in their own direction to "deliver a cohesive computing experience." Solus is "ready to go" for developers, gamers, and content creators.
