The team at ULOSINO ("ULOSINO", "our", "us") takes your privacy seriously. When you use the ULOSINO web service ("the service"), you should consider the following notice - if you do not agree to the ULOSINO Privacy Notice, do not use the service.

---

### Data Collection

ULOSINO does not collect personally identifying information through the service.

When you access the service, you are downloading data from the software hosted on a server ("delivery software"). Most delivery software used by the service is open source and publicly available for copy or study.

Below is an illustrative list of the data delivery software may collect:

- The date and time when you access the service;
- The web browser name (for example, Chrome) and version;
- The operating system name (for example, Windows) and version;
- The values of operating system accessibility and colour mode preferences, and;
- The operating system language display method (for example, right-to-left).

Data collected by delivery software is collected automatically in order to provide additional features and/or deliver the service without error (for example when using an older browser that may not support the requested feature).

Data collected by delivery software is not transmitted to ULOSINO.

The service uses <Link href="https://plausible.io">Plausible Analytics</Link> ("analytics") to measure the community engagement and performance of the service.

Below is an illustrative list of the data analytics may collect:

- Your Internet Protocol (IP) address;
- The date and time you access the service;
- Length of your usage of the service;
- Region information;
- The web browser name (for example, Chrome);
- The operating system name (for example, Windows);
- The device type (for example, Desktop), and;
- Referring website and exit point.

The analytics is compliant with the European Union General Data Protection Regulation and other similar legal regulations and treaties enforced in other regions of the world. All data collected by analytics is anonymous and cannot be used to track you across multiple visits, or across web services not owned or operated by ULOSINO. Reference the <Link href="https://plausible.io/data-policy">Plausible Analytics Data Policy</Link> for more information.

### Tracking and Cookies

The service does not track you across the service or across web services not owned or operated by ULOSINO.

The service does not store cookies, nor track you using cookies.

---

### Data Retention

ULOSINO may retain collected data for as long as there is a legitimate business interest.

When using the service, the disk storage of the your computer may be used to store display preferences, provide features, or cache the service for offline availability. This data is stored on the computer until it expires, or unless cleared manually.

---

### Security

Requests to the service are sent using HTTPS/SSL and TLS. Additionally, the service uses a Content Security Policy to protect the service, when delivered to you, from XSS and code injection.

Requests, as handled by our service delivery partner Vercel, are compliant with the European Union General Data Protection Regulation and the SOC2 corporate framework.

If you access these services, you are no longer using the service and are bound by the legal documentation of that third-party service.
