## Definitions

The "Service" refers to the ULOSINO web service, and all content, services, and products provided by ULOSINO at or through ulosino.com, or any subdomains of ulosino.com.

"ULOSINO" refers to the data controller, owner, and operator of the ULOSINO web service, as listed in the Licence. "Third-Party" refers to data controllers, and owners or operators other than ULOSINO.

"You," and "Your" refers to the individual that has visited or is using the Service.

"Request" refers to when You download or upload data from remote servers. "Request Host" refers to Your electronic device that facilitates Requests. "Delivery Software" refers to software that Requests interface with, and is hosted on a remote server.

## Data Collection, Usage, &amp; Retention

### Through the Service

ULOSINO does not collect data that could identify You through the Service.

### Through Delivery Software

Below is an illustrative list of the data Delivery Software may collect:

- The date and time when You access the Service;
- The web browser name (for example, Chrome) and version;
- The operating system name (for example, Windows) and version;
- The values of operating system accessibility and colour mode preferences, and;
- The operating system language display method (for example, right-to-left).

Data collected by Delivery Software is collected from Your Request Host in order to provide additional features and/or provide the Service without error (for example, when using an older browser that may not support the requested feature).

Data collected by Delivery Software is not sent to ULOSINO. The data is retained for the duration of Your Request(s).

### Through Plausible Analytics

The Service uses <Link href="https://plausible.io">Plausible Analytics</Link>, operated by <Link href="https://plausible.io/imprint">Plausible Insights OÜ</Link>, to measure Your engagement with the Service. Below is an illustrative list of the data Plausible Analytics may collect:

- Your Internet Protocol address;
- The date and time You access the Service;
- Length of Your usage of the Service;
- Approximated region information;
- The web browser name (for example, Chrome) and version;
- The operating system name (for example, Windows) and version, and;
- The device type (for example, Desktop).

Plausible Analytics is compliant with the European Union General Data Protection Regulation. All data collected by Plausible Analytics is anonymous and cannot be used to track You across multiple visits, or across Third-Party web services. Reference the <Link href="https://plausible.io/data-policy">Plausible Analytics Data Policy</Link> for more information.

ULOSINO owns the data collected for the Service through Plausible Analytics. The data is sent to ULOSINO. The data will be retained for the duration of ULOSINO's paid partnership with Plausible Insights OÜ, and for 24 months afterwards.

### Retention by the Request Host

When using the Service, the disk storage of Your Request Host may be used to store display preferences, provide features, or cache the Service for offline availability. This data is stored on the Request Host until it expires, or unless cleared manually.

When using the Service, Third-Party hyperlinks may be presented to You. Unless otherwise disclosed, third-party links act only as a convenience to You and are not a part of a monetary agreement between the Third-Party and ULOSINO.
